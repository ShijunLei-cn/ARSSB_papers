## Subject
economics

## Research Direction
Examine how institutions (e.g., welfare states, labor market regulations) moderate the effect of trade openness on income inequality.

## Core Hypothesis
The paper expects that trade openness has no average effect on income inequality in the full sample, but heterogeneous effects by development level: decreasing inequality in developing countries and increasing inequality in developed countries, consistent with the Stolper-Samuelson theorem.
