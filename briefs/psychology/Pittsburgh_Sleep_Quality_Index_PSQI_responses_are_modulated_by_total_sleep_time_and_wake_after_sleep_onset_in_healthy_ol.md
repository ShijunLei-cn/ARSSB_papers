## Research Direction
Longitudinal studies to better understand determinants of sleep quality and improve prediction of perceived sleep quality

## Core Hypothesis
Based on prior literature, the authors hypothesized that sleep efficiency (SE) would be associated with PSQI-reported sleep disruption, and that sleep onset latency (SOL) would be a main predictor of perceived sleep quality in the elderly.

## Methodology Template
- **Design**: Empirical analysis guided by: What are the specific objective sleep drivers behind older adult responses to subjective measures in the PSQI?
