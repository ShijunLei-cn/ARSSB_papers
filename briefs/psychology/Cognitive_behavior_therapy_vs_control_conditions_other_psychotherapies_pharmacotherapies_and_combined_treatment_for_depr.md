## Research Direction

## Core Hypothesis
The paper expected CBT to be effective compared to control conditions, with moderate to large effect sizes, and to be comparable or superior to other active treatments. Previous meta-analyses (e.g., Cuijpers et al., 2013) had found CBT effective, but the current study aimed to update and expand these findings.
