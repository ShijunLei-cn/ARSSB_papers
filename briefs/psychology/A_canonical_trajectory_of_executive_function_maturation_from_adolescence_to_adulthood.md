## Research Direction
Investigate the role of pubertal development in driving executive function changes during early adolescence.

## Core Hypothesis
The paper expected to find a non-linear developmental trajectory of executive function with rapid development in late childhood to mid-adolescence, smaller changes through mid-adolescence, and stabilization to adult levels in late adolescence, consistent with theoretical models.
